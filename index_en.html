What is Mallangi?

Mallangi (Balloon Stress Ball) is a sensory toy made by filling specially designed high-elasticity latex balloons with soft filler. The unique pressure and restorative force when squeezed provide an ASMR effect that stabilizes the mind.

When you need focus, when stress builds up, or when you're just bored, touch Mallangi. The healing that starts from your fingertips spreads throughout your body.
